Return Policy Overview

At Audentia, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not satisfied, we offer a comprehensive return and exchange policy to ensure your shopping experience exceeds expectations.

Return Window

You may return most items within 30 days of the delivery date for a full refund or exchange. Items must be in their original condition with all tags attached and original packaging included.

Extended Return Period

Items purchased during our holiday season (November 1 - December 31) may be returned until January 31 of the following year.

Eligible Items for Return

The following items are eligible for return or exchange:

  • Unworn clothing with original tags attached
  • Accessories in original condition and packaging
  • Footwear in original condition with original box
  • Items that do not fit properly
  • Items received in damaged condition
  • Items that differ significantly from the product description

Non-Returnable Items

For hygiene and safety reasons, the following items cannot be returned:

  • Underwear and intimate apparel
  • Swimwear (unless defective)
  • Items marked as "Final Sale"
  • Custom or personalized items
  • Items damaged by normal wear and tear
  • Items returned after the 30-day return window
  • Items without original tags or packaging

Return Process

Step 1: Initiate Your Return

To start your return, please:

  • Log into your account and go to "Order History"
  • Select the order containing the item(s) you wish to return
  • Click "Return Items" and follow the prompts
  • Alternatively, contact our customer service team

Step 2: Prepare Your Package

  • Include the original packing slip or return form
  • Pack items securely in their original packaging when possible
  • Ensure all tags are still attached
  • Include any accessories that came with the item

Step 3: Ship Your Return

  • Use the prepaid return label provided (for qualifying returns)
  • Drop off at any authorized shipping location
  • Keep your tracking receipt for your records

Return Shipping

Free Return Shipping

We provide free return shipping for:

  • Defective or damaged items
  • Items that don't match the product description
  • Wrong items sent by mistake
  • Orders over $50 (within the US)

Return Shipping Fees

A $5.95 return shipping fee will be deducted from your refund for:

  • Change of mind returns
  • Size or color exchanges
  • Orders under $50
  • International returns

Exchanges

Size and Color Exchanges

We offer free exchanges for different sizes or colors of the same item, subject to availability. If the new item has a different price, you will be charged or refunded the difference.

Exchange Process

  1. Follow the return process above
  2. Select "Exchange" and choose your preferred size/color
  3. We'll send your new item once we receive your return
  4. If your preferred item is unavailable, we'll issue a refund

Refund Processing

Refund Timeline

  • Processing begins once we receive your returned item
  • Inspection typically takes 1-2 business days
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method
  • Credit card refunds: 3-5 business days
  • PayPal refunds: 1-3 business days
  • Bank transfers: 5-10 business days

Refund Amount

Your refund will include:

  • The full purchase price of returned items
  • Original shipping costs (if the return is our error)
  • Applicable taxes

Return shipping fees will be deducted from your refund when applicable.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item:

  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  • Provide photos of the damage or defect
  • We'll provide a prepaid return label
  • You'll receive a full refund including original shipping
  • We can also send a replacement if preferred

International Returns

International customers may return items within 30 days, subject to the following:

  • Customer is responsible for return shipping costs
  • Items must be returned via trackable shipping method
  • Customer is responsible for any customs duties or fees
  • Refunds will be processed in the original currency

Gift Returns

Items purchased as gifts can be returned by the recipient with the gift receipt or order number. Gift returns will be refunded via store credit unless the original purchaser provides authorization for a refund to the original payment method.

Store Credit

In some cases, we may issue store credit instead of a refund:

  • Returns without original receipt
  • Items returned outside the return window (at our discretion)
  • Gift returns without purchaser authorization

Store credit has no expiration date and can be used for future purchases.

Return Status Updates

Track your return status through:

  • Your online account dashboard
  • Email notifications at each step
  • Customer service team

Customer Service

Our customer service team is here to help with any return or exchange questions:

  • Email: returns@audentia.com
  • Response time: Within 24 hours
  • Include: Order number, item details, and reason for return

Special Circumstances

Pre-Order Items

Pre-order items can be cancelled before shipping. Once shipped, standard return policy applies.

Sale Items

Items purchased on sale are eligible for return under the same conditions, unless marked as "Final Sale."

Promotional Items

Free promotional items must be returned with any qualifying purchase being returned.
